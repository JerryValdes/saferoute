About SafeRoute

SafeRoute shows live, neighborhood-level safety scores powered by official open police data. Use it as one input alongside your own research and judgment.

How scores work

Each crime report is weighted by severity, then aggregated per neighborhood:

  • Weight 5: Homicide, rape, sexual assault
  • Weight 4: Armed robbery, aggravated assault, carjacking
  • Weight 3: Robbery, assault, battery
  • Weight 2: Burglary, motor vehicle theft, arson
  • Weight 1: Theft, larceny

The weighted sum is converted to a 1–5 score using a log scale calibrated per city: score = 5 − 2.5 × log₁₀(weighted / baseline), clamped to 1–5. Neighborhoods scoring ≥ 3.5 are Safe, 2.5–3.5 are Use Caution, below 2.5 are Avoid.

Data sources & update frequency

Crime scores refresh from live APIs every 6 hours and cover the most recent 12 months of reported incidents. Neighborhood boundaries are cached for 7 days.

About the descriptions

Hand-written neighborhood descriptions are editorial assessments for tourists, informed by crime data, published travel guides, and general knowledge. They may not reflect very recent changes in neighborhood character. Always check current local advisories.

SafeRoute is provided for informational purposes only. Crime statistics describe historical patterns, not guarantees of future safety. Always exercise personal judgment when traveling.
